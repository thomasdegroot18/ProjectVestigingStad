Registration

Teamcaptains have to register you. Please ask your local dance society for the contact information of your teams teamcaptain. If you have trouble finding your teamcaptains contact us

There are 600 spots for the tournament. The deadline for registation will be anounced. After the registration the system will draft names. For couples who are register together, you will get in together. Whe are using the system that was also used in previous ETDS (ETDS Groningen). The cancelation policy will be anounced.

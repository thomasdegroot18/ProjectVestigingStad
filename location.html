Location

Sportcentrum Universum
Science Park 306, 1098 XH Amsterdam

How to get there by car

By car - A1

Use the right lane to take the Ring-Oost/A10/E35 exit toward Rotterdam/Luchthaven Schiphol, then keep right, follow signs for Watergraafsmeer.

By car - A2

At the interchange Knooppunt Amstel, Use the right lanes to follow signs for E35/A10 toward Zaanstad. Then take exit s113 to Watergraafsmeer.

How to get there by public transport

We strongly advise you to plan your trip with 9292.nl (also available in english) and ns.nl. Here you can find all the information about which platforms you'll have to be.

Train

There are 3 stations near to the USC. Depending on where you traveling from you can choose between Science park, Muiderpoort or Amstel. From Muiderpoort and Amstel Station you can than take a bus to the USC.

From Amsterdam Central / Science Park
If your traveling from amsterdam central, we recommend to take the train to Amsterdam Science park. It is only 2 stops from central.
From science park it is only 10 minutes walk to the USC Universum.

From Amsterdam central it is only two trainstops to Amsterdam Science park.

Bus (From Amstel station or Muiderpoort station)

From Amstel station and Muiderpoort station you can get the bus 40 to Muiderpoort station respectivetly to Amstel station. The busstop is Science Park Aqua, which is in front of the USC Universum. At the Amstelstation you can also take bus 240, which also stops at science Park Aqua.

Bike (from Amstel station or Muiderpoort station)

You can also hire a public transport bike at Amstel station and Muiderpoort station. The bike costs 3.85 eur/24 hours for the first 72 hours. Parking

We have limited parking available at the location Science Park. It is possible to park around the Science Park, but it will be payed parking. Our tip is to park outside amsterdam to try circumvent paying expensive parking rates. Here are some possible locations:

P+R Weesp/Leeuwenveld.
374 parkingspots
Click here for location on Google Maps.
From here it is a 9 min train ride to Amsterdam Science Park.
Due to fusion with Amsterdam, the parkingfees in Weesp can change, so be sure to check parking rates before you park

P+R Diemen
128 parkingspots

From here it is a 2 min train ride to Science Park.

P+R Almere Muziekwijk
345 parkingspots
Click here for location on Google Maps.
From here it is a 19 min train ride to Science Park.

Mandatory Shifts

A large event as the ETDS needs a lot of helping hands. Without people helping out ETDS's couldn't take place. This is why competitors are mandatory to do a minimal amount of hours for shifts.

Volunteer shifts

Besides the mandatory shifts we can use every other shift hours you are willing to offer us!
With doing volunteer shifts beside your mandatory shift you can also earn bonuspoints for your team!



It is not possible to inscribe for shifts yet.
